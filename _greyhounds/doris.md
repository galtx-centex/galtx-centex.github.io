I come from a land down under.

Doris is probably more energetic than your typical couch potato.  She loves to go for long walks a couple times per day and is a good leash walker.  She would love to be with an active family. However, Doris does have a high prey drive, so no smaller pets or young children please.  The flip side to that is, she gets on fine with the male greyhounds in her foster home, and has shown no space issues with the adult humans.  Dorey hasn't had any thunder-phobia or other noise reactivity either, she even does fine on the smooth floors.  She is super sweet and easy going, enjoys soft toys, and really likes to spread out and make herself comfortable when indoors. 

Her stunning dark brindle coat, and floppy "ear hat" draw admiring looks wherever she goes.
