Nova has the potential to be a real super star! This stunning young girl was found stray in West Texas. She is playful and cuddly, outspoken and brave. She crates well while her foster is at work, but would love it if you invited her to share your sofa.  The vet estimates she is only one year old, so although full grown, she has a lot of puppy-like behavior.  She will need to be redirected at times, but some boundaries and training will be rewarded with a stellar companion.
