Johnny is our latest Aussie lad. He is quite the looker with a hint of blue showing through his fawn coat. Johnny carries himself with confidence, tackiling interior stairs on day one, equally happy lounging in a crate or free-roaming, while his foster family is at work.  He has not shown any fear over the frequent thunderstorms we have had lately. Gets along well with other greyhounds or dogs of similar size, but shows a high prey drive toward little dogs and cats.
