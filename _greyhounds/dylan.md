Dylan is Texas born and raised! He is tattooed and registered, but no racing history to his name. Dylan has a handsome dark mask and black overcoat giving him a unique look. He is a smart and gregarious boy, who is quickly getting the hang of city living. He loves his walks, and is friendly to everyone he encounters including children and smaller dogs.  He is passionate about his meals and will gently take a treat from your hand.  Dylan entertains us every afternoon with a case of the zoomies! He currently lives peacefully with a grumpy female greyhound and male mixed breed dog.  He has experience going up and down stairs with ease, but does not like slippery tile floors.
