Oreo is beautiful girl who is an absolute joy to have around. She has a calm and composed demeanor around people and other dogs. Although she can be shy and reserved indoors, her personality transforms in the backyard where she becomes an avid squirrel chaser. She loves to run and play outside! Oreo gets along great with little IG foster brother. She has never shown any aggression toward him, even around food. Fully house-trained, she will relax in her open crate for a little down time, then follow her people around the house. Oreo loves going for walks and exploring new places, but that is balanced by her laid-back style inside the home. She really has been an ideal foster.
