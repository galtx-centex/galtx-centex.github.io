Thank you for visiting
GALT-Central Texas!

We are thrilled to announce that GPA-Centex is now an official chapter of
Greyhound Adoption League of Texas, Inc.

GPA-CT is now an official chapter of GALT, Inc.

This is a big change for us, as we've been a chapter of Greyhound Pets of America for more than twenty years. We are proud to join GALT as we believe that being a part of their organization will be the most effective way to achieve our mission: to help more greyhounds find loving homes. We'll now be known as GALT-Central Texas. Read more here.

We are passionate about Greyhounds and our mission is to find the perfect home for these beautiful, sweet, gentle dogs. Please take a few minutes to explore our site and learn about Greyhounds. We hope you are inspired to learn more and will contact us soon.
