If you have a pending adoption or foster application...

Please know that there is a shortage of available greyhounds at this time. Florida tracks have until the end of 2020 to shut down operations. Owners are racing their dogs as long as they can, while breeders have, for the most part, stopped breeding. We are doing everything we can to get in more dogs; they are coming up slowly. We currently have a waiting list, so incoming dogs are not likely “available” dogs. They are fulfilling existing approved applications. Applications are processed according to when they were received AND provided there is a good match (children, other pets, activity level.)

We will keep you updated as things change. We are excited to see such enthusiasm for the greyhounds, and we appreciate your patience.
